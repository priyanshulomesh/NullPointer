Coding Round Based:

  • There will be 1-2 coding rounds in which shortlisting will be done on the basis of number of questions completed and time, and also CGPA if clash happens.

  • After the coding round there will be 1-3 interview rounds, in which questions will be asked based on DSA, projects and on your core subjects. After shortlisting is done in these rounds there maybe a HR round.

  • Requirements and things to take care of:

    • To maintain above 7.5 CGPA with no active backlog and to be able to take part in coding rounds.

Hackathon Based:

  • There can be an optional coding round in which shortlisting will be done on the basis of number of questions completed and time, and also CGPA if clash happens.

  • There will be an idea submission round during which you have to form a team(if it is team based) and prepare a presentation based on what you are going to make. There can be a single theme, multiple themes or no themes at all on the basis of which you have to develop your ideas.

  • After getting shortlisted in idea submission round there will be 1-2 development rounds where you have to develop your prototype(there can be a mentor assigned to you). Those who will develop good prototype and present their ideas well to the panel of judges will be selected for interview round.

  • The interview process will be same as coding round based selection but can be lineant.

  • Requirements and things to take care of:

    • To maintain above 7.5 CGPA with no active backlog and to be able to take part in coding rounds.
    • To nurture and hone your development skills.
